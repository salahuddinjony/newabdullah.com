<!-- Long Bio -->
<section class="bio-section long-bio">
  <h2>Long</h2>
  <p>
    I am currently working as a <strong>LLM Architect</strong> at <strong>Aisera</strong>, leading the development of foundational models for Large Language Models (LLMs) tailored to automate enterprise-level tasks. My day-to-day involves training and fine-tuning models with advanced reasoning capabilities to handle real-world dialogue, ticketing systems, customer workflows, and more. The aim is to make AI not only intelligent but also contextually aware and responsive.
  </p>
  <p>
    Before this, I worked as a <strong>Machine Learning Engineer</strong> at <strong>Meta</strong>. My contributions were central to the <strong>Ads Ranking</strong> ecosystem and personalized shopping experiences. Specifically, I was part of the <strong>Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns</strong> team, where we utilized deep learning models to enhance ad relevance and engagement at scale. I also had the opportunity to be part of the Gen AI team working on <strong>LLaMA</strong>, Meta’s open-source LLM library.
  </p>
  <p>
    My academic journey includes a <strong>Ph.D.</strong> from <em>Florida International University</em> and a Master’s from <em>King Fahd University of Petroleum & Minerals</em>. During my research, I worked on AI-powered drug recommendation systems and high-dimensional feature selection — leveraging platforms like <code>TensorFlow</code> and <code>Deeplearning4j</code> to solve medical data challenges.
  </p>
  <p>
    I hold multiple certifications in <strong>machine learning</strong> and <strong>cloud technologies</strong> and am driven by a passion for leveraging AI to bring measurable, human-centric value to the world.
  </p>
</section>
