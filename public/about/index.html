Long

I am currently working as a LLM Architect at Aisera, leading the development of foundational models for Large Language Models (LLMs) tailored to automate enterprise-level tasks. My day-to-day involves training and fine-tuning models with advanced reasoning capabilities to handle real-world dialogue, ticketing systems, customer workflows, and more. The aim is to make AI not only intelligent but also contextually aware and responsive.

Before this, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at Meta. My contributions were central to the Ads Ranking ecosystem and personalized shopping experiences. Specifically, I was part of the Advantage+ Shopping Campaigns team, where we utilized deep learning models to enhance ad relevance and engagement at scale. I also had the opportunity to be part of the Gen AI team working on LLaMA, Meta’s open-source LLM library.

My academic journey includes a Ph.D. from Florida International University and a Master’s from King Fahd University of Petroleum & Minerals. During my research, I worked on AI-powered drug recommendation systems and high-dimensional feature selection — leveraging platforms like TensorFlow and Deeplearning4j to solve medical data challenges.

I hold multiple certifications in machine learning and cloud technologies and am driven by a passion for leveraging AI to bring measurable, human-centric value to the world.
